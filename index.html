About

Here is our story

  • 2018

    An Idea Emmereged

    One cold morning two college friends, Ruben and Sipan, were sitting in class as Ruben craved a warm cup of coffee to wake him up. Sipan prefered tea but agreed, that a warm beverage would really help start his day.

  • May 2019

    A Brand was Born

    We decided to start putting together some products and selling it to other tired classmates. The idea was being implemented but on a small scale.

  • December 2019

    Transition to Fully Online

    After months of working with suppliers and web developers the offically launched the website at the end of 2019. We did over $100,000 in sales in just the first month of going online!

  • July 2020

    Expansion

    Due to the success of our brand going online, we managed to move our supply into a clean and spacious warehouse. With all the space we have, we are now able to have a bigger quantity and a bigger variety of products for you to enjoy!
